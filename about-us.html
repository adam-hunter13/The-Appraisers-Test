About The Appraisers

Founded in 1978, The Appraisers has a staff of 2 Commercial and 1 Residential Certified Appraisers with combined appraisal experience of more than 65 years. The Appraisers is a well established appraisal firm with expertise in several important areas. We accept assignments of all kinds but we specialize in 3 basic areas: 1) rural land (including farms, ranches and recreational property, 2) Commercial land and buildings (Including those found in rural areas of the state) and 3) Real property tax valuations for County Assessors and for property owners who choose to appeal their valuations.

Gordon E Lowe, Certified General Appraiser

Gordon began appraising in 1991. Gordon's expertise is in appraising agricultural/recreational properties including conservation easements, water rights and open lands. He also provides commercial valuations for numerous County Assessors and private property owners.

Jeff Salmon, Certified General Appraiser

Jeff joined our team in 2019. He has been appraising a wide variety of commercial, agricultural, and residential properties since 2008. Jeff began his appraisal career in Lethbridge, Alberta and received his AACI designation from the Appraisal Institute of Canada in 2011. Jeff specializes in residential, agricultural and rural commercial appraisals.
